Level Guide — A1 → C2

What to focus on at each CEFR level.

  • A1Alphabet, greetings, pronouns, numbers, simple present. Use DW Nicos Weg + Anja.
  • A2Past tense, modal verbs, accusative/dative. LearnGermanOriginal + DW.
  • B1Full cases, Konjunktiv II, complex sentences. Easy German + Slow German.
  • B2Passive, advanced grammar, authentic media. Start German YouTube/Netflix.
  • C1Nuance, academic German, complex argumentation. Read German literature.
  • C2Near-native fluency. Kafka, Goethe, Schiller in original German.
